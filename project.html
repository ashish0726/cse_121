About Our School

Ashish Tiwari School is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering the development of well-rounded students. Since its establishment, we have been committed to academic excellence and character development.

Our experienced faculty members are passionate about teaching and mentoring students to achieve their full potential. We believe in creating a supportive learning environment where every student can thrive and succeed.

With state-of-the-art facilities and modern teaching methods, we prepare students for challenges of the future while instilling values of integrity, discipline, and social responsibility.

Our Courses

📚 Science & Technology

Explore the wonders of science through hands-on experiments and modern laboratory facilities. Learn physics, chemistry, and biology in an interactive environment.

🔢 Mathematics & Logic

Develop problem-solving skills and mathematical thinking. Our curriculum covers arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and advanced mathematical concepts.

📖 Languages & Literature

Master English and other languages with expert instructors. Enhance communication skills through reading, writing, and literature study.

🎨 Arts & Creativity

Express yourself through various art forms including drawing, painting, music, and dance. Develop creative thinking and artistic appreciation.
